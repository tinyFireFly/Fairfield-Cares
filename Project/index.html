Function

The Fairfield CARES Community Coalition brings together citizens, educators and leaders from a broad spectrum of the Fairfield Community in support of our shared vision of a community that promotes healthy, responsible choices through prevention and education for youth, young adults, and families.

Meetings

The Fairfield CARES Community Coalition meets the 3rd Wednesday of every month from 8:30-10:00 AM at 501 Kings Hwy East, in the 2nd Floor FPS Board of Education Conference Room, and via Zoom. Please check the Town Meetings Calendar for cancellations or location changes.

The Hub CT

The Hub CT is our partner in prevention as the state-designated Regional Behavioral Health Action Organization (RBHAO) serving Southwestern Connecticut — the 14 communities from Greenwich to Stratford. The Hub's role is to serve as a strategic partner and resource for the region, supporting and coordinating initiatives related to mental health, suicide, substance misuse, and problem gambling, and liaising between the region and the state. Visit their website and subscribe to the Hub's highly informative newsletter.
